Marvel Character Profiles
Home Comics About

Spider-Man

Peter Parker, the friendly neighborhood Spider-Man, balancing hero life with responsibility.

Iron Man

Tony Stark, genius, billionaire, and armored Avenger with a sharp wit.

Logan / Wolverine

Mutant with healing factor, adamantium claws, and an untamed spirit.

Captain America

Steve Rogers, the super-soldier who embodies hope, courage, and leadership.

Black Widow

Natasha Romanoff, elite spy and Avenger, skilled in combat and espionage.

Hulk

Bruce Banner’s rage unleashes the unstoppable green giant, the Hulk.

Thor

The God of Thunder, wielding Mjolnir and defending realms with honor.

Mr. Fantastic

Reed Richards, leader of the Fantastic Four, with unmatched intellect and elasticity.

Invisible Woman

Susan Storm, powerful and compassionate, master of invisibility and force fields.

Human Torch

Johnny Storm, hot-headed and fiery hero of the Fantastic Four.

The Thing

Ben Grimm, rock-skinned powerhouse of the Fantastic Four with a heart of gold.

Storm

Ororo Munroe, mutant queen of weather manipulation and a true leader of the X-Men.

Cyclops

Scott Summers, X-Men leader with optic blasts and unwavering discipline.

Jean Grey

Mutant telepath and telekinetic, carrying the fiery power of the Phoenix.

Laura Kinney

Also known as X-23, clone of Wolverine with claws and resilience to match.

Ghost Spider

Gwen Stacy from another universe, taking on the mantle of Spider-Woman.

Kate Bishop

Young Avenger with unmatched archery skills and a sharp tongue, heir to Hawkeye’s legacy.
